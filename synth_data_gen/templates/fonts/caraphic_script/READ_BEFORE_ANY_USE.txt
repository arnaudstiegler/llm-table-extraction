Caraphic Script marries the crisp elegance of script typefaces with the beauty of Middle Eastern design. 
Its strokes, bold and assertive, dance with a weightiness that commands attention, yet it retains a sharp clarity that ensures readability. While undeniably decorative, there's an inherent professional balance in its design, making it a top pick for logotypes and branding. 
Its unique character emerges from its seamless support for both Latin and Arabic scripts, bridging worlds and cultures in a single, harmonious typeface. This isn't just a font; it's a global statement, speaking to audiences far and wide with unmatched sophistication.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and has a guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures, and more features; all to give you full control and customizability. It has extensive lingual support, covering all Arabic and Latin-based languages, from Northern Europe to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers.

Caraphic Script's unmatched beauty and versatility are crafted with precision by Mans Greback. It stands as a testament to his skill in intertwining emotion, culture, and utility within typography. With each font he creates, Greback solidifies his reputation for producing designs that resonate deeply while providing exceptional functionality for professionals everywhere.
