Viceroy is a stylish modern calligraphy font with casual chic flair. It is perfect for branding, wedding invites and cards, and maybe for red wine label.

Viceroy includes full set of gorgeous uppercase and lowercase letters, and numerals, a large range of punctuation . All lowercase letters include beginning and ending swashes, giving realistic hand-lettered style.
